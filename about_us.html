About Midtown Motors

Originally founded in 1997 by two brothers, Bob and Jacob Marley, MidTown Motors has humble beginnings with the two brothers bringing their passion for selling together with ther love of cars and using a small commercial lot in the centre of their home town and nothing more than 3 specially selected cars - MidTown Motors was officially opened.










As is the risk with only 3 carefully selected vintage cars, business at first was slow but through tenacity and ensuing the highest level af care and customer service, Bob and Jacob soon found word had spread and demand for their cars soon increased.










Setting themselves apart form their competitors by ensuring each car was scrutinised and carefully selected, and by consistetly delivering the highest level of customer care, the Marley brothers had extended their car selection to consistently having over 20 cars to select from by the year 2000. From the humble beginnings, a thriving business was emerging.










Growing to meet the community and customer needs proved to a a difficult and rewarding challenge for the Marley brothers, and they soon found a space in their venture for someone who shared the same passion and integrity for for exceptional customer service as them.





In 2001, Ebeneezer Scrooge joined the MidTown motors team as Chief Operating Officer. Sharing the same vision and passion for customer success as Bob and Jacob, Ebeneezer brought a youthful exuberance and connection to a younger demographic, enabling MidTown Motors to deliver a high quality service to an even wider range of people and further serve their community.








MidTown Motors' foundation has always been strong community service and the company prides itself in it's many local sponsorships, offering financial aid and support to local clubs and initiatives including Tidy Towns, several local sports teams and the local youth centre aiming to provide young people with a safe location where young people can socialise and learn new skills that will positively contribute to both themselves and their community.










Now having grown to a dedicated staff of over 35 employees and over 150 exceptional quality vehicles, the team at Midtown Motros prides itself in refusing to compromise on the experience of the customer or the quality of vehicles on offer. Only with exceptional love and dedication to serving the local community can the MidTown Motors experience be built.
